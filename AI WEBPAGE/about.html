Our Story

Crafting Comfort Since Day One

RD SOFA MAKER is a trusted sofa manufacturer located at Mukai Chowk, Vikas Nagar Road, Kiwale – 412101.

Owned by Harish Vishwakarma and Deepak Vishwakarma, we specialize in custom sofa making with premium materials and affordable pricing.

We focus on comfort, durability, and modern designs tailored to customer needs. Every sofa is handcrafted in our factory with solid wood frames, high-density foam, and premium upholstery fabrics.

Our mission is simple – deliver factory-quality sofas at factory-direct prices, cutting out the middleman so you get the best value for your money.
