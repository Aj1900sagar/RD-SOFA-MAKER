🛋️ Our Story

Building Dreams, One Sofa at a Time

RD Sofa Maker was founded with a simple vision – to provide premium quality, custom-made sofas at factory direct pricing, eliminating the middlemen and making luxury affordable for every home.

Located at Mukai Chowk, Kiwale – 412101, Pune, our manufacturing unit is equipped with modern tools and experienced craftsmen who have been perfecting the art of sofa making for over a decade.

We specialize in creating bespoke sofas tailored to your exact needs – from L-shape sectionals for spacious living rooms to compact 2-seaters for cozy apartments. Every sofa is built with high-density foam, solid teak or sheesham wood frames, and premium upholstery fabrics sourced from the best suppliers.

Our commitment to quality, comfort, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of 500+ families across Pune and neighboring areas.
